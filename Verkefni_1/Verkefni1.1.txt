1. Finding the quickest route through a supermarket
while in supermarket
	walk through aisle
	was the product in the aisle
		if yes
			go get product
			and leave supermarket
		else
			go to next aisle
	

2. Generate Fibonacci numbers

	1. start with a pair of numbers
	2. add those two numbers together to get a new number
	3. the new number is now the current 
	4. add the old number to the current number to get a new number
	5. repeat 3 and 4
